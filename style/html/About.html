About Khmer Art Gallery

Preserving and promoting Cambodia's rich artistic heritage for future generations

Our Story

Khmer Art Gallery was founded with a singular mission: to celebrate and preserve the extraordinary artistic legacy of Cambodia. Our journey began in 2015 when a group of passionate art historians and collectors came together to create a platform that would showcase the beauty and depth of Khmer art to the world. From ancient Angkorian sculptures to contemporary paintings, we curate collections that tell the story of Cambodia's artistic evolution across centuries. Each piece in our galleries has been carefully selected to represent the skill, creativity, and cultural significance of Khmer artisans. Today, we collaborate with museums, galleries, and cultural institutions across Cambodia to provide visitors with an unforgettable journey through the nation's artistic heritage.
